What is this site?

There are 3 big reasons I want to develop this site in the way that it is:

  • Primarily, I want to document my progress in a (hopefully) interesting medium for other people (or even my future self) to come find. I guess I'm almost hoping this will end up being a time capsule of sorts!
  • Secondly, this site is here as a resource for other people that are interested in the same things I am. If I help anyone out of a programatic jam through the writings on this site, I will be very happy!
  • Finally, and most superficially, I want this as a resource for future employers to see how passionate I am about this field, and my competence in the field. The current job market (as of 10/18/2024) is quite bad, so standing out in any way is very important. Hopefully, this will make a difference!
